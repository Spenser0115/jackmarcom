About Me

When Jack Marcom was a young boy, he lived vicariously through the films and tv shows he watched. That medium fostered his love for storytelling, and he began to chase after his dream of being a screenwriter. His aspirations led him to Liberty University where he acquired a B.F.A. in film production and content development. While attending Liberty, Jack had the pleasure of working on many projects, allowing him to experience the vast array of different positions on a film set, like Set Decorator, HMU, Props, and AD. Outside of school, Jack co-founded a production company, “Beargoat Films”, where he helps to write, direct, and produce feature and short films. Jack is currently working on multiple writing projects, most notably a feature film script he will be co-directing this summer.
